Risk Disclosure Statement

1. CFD Trading Risks

Contracts for Difference (CFDs) are leveraged products. This means a small deposit can give you much larger market exposure. While leverage can magnify profits, it also magnifies losses which can exceed your initial deposit.

2. Leverage Risk

Velox offers leverage up to 1:2000 on Forex pairs. At this leverage, a 0.05% adverse price movement can result in a 100% loss of your margin. Always use stop losses when trading with leverage.

3. Market Risk

Financial markets are subject to rapid and unpredictable price movements caused by economic data, political events, natural disasters, and market sentiment. Past performance is not indicative of future results.

4. Cryptocurrency Risk

Cryptocurrency markets are highly volatile and largely unregulated. Prices can move 20–50% in a single day. Regulatory changes in any jurisdiction can significantly impact crypto asset values.

5. Liquidity Risk

During periods of extreme market volatility, spreads may widen significantly and orders may not be filled at the requested price. This is known as slippage.

6. Technology Risk

Trading platforms, internet connections, and electronic systems can fail. You should have contingency plans for accessing your account during platform outages.

Velox is committed to preventing money laundering, terrorist financing, and other financial crimes.

1. KYC Requirements

All clients must complete Know Your Customer (KYC) verification before making withdrawals. Required documents include a government-issued photo ID (passport or national ID card) and proof of address (utility bill or bank statement less than 3 months old).

2. Source of Funds

For deposits exceeding $1,000, we may request documentation proving the legitimate source of funds. This may include salary slips, bank statements, or business ownership documents.

3. Suspicious Activity

We monitor all transactions for suspicious patterns including unusual deposit/withdrawal frequency, transactions inconsistent with trading activity, and deposits from multiple sources. Suspicious activity is reported to relevant authorities.

4. PEP Screening

Politically Exposed Persons (PEPs) and individuals on international sanctions lists are subject to enhanced due diligence and may be refused service.

5. Record Keeping

We maintain records of all transactions and KYC documentation for a minimum of 5 years in accordance with international standards.

Order Execution Policy

1. Execution Model

Velox operates as a Straight-Through Processing (STP) broker. Your orders are passed directly to liquidity providers without a dealing desk. We do not take the opposite side of your trades.

2. Execution Speed

Under normal market conditions, market orders are executed within 25 milliseconds. During periods of high volatility or low liquidity, execution times may increase.

3. Slippage

Slippage may occur when the market moves between the time you place an order and when it is executed. This can be positive or negative. We implement best execution practices to minimise negative slippage.

4. Order Types

We currently offer Market Orders only. All orders are filled at the best available market price at the time of execution.

5. Trading Hours

Forex and Metals: 24 hours, Monday–Friday. Cryptocurrencies: 24/7. Indices and Stocks: Follow exchange hours. Spreads may be wider outside regular trading hours.
