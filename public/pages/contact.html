Quick Answers
How long do deposits take?
BTC takes ~1 hour, USDT is instant, ETH takes ~30 minutes. After blockchain confirmation, balance is credited immediately.
My withdrawal is pending — when?
Withdrawals are processed within 24 hours during business days. KYC verification is required before your first withdrawal.
I can't log into my account
Try resetting your password using the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. If you still can't access, contact us with your registered email.
KYC documents rejected — what now?
Re-submit with clearer images ensuring all corners are visible and text is legible. Check the rejection email for specific reasons.
