FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How do I deposit funds into my Velox account?
We accept Bitcoin (BTC), USDT (TRC20), and Ethereum (ETH). After registering, go to your dashboard, click Deposit, select your preferred cryptocurrency, and send the funds to the displayed address. Deposits are credited within 30 minutes of blockchain confirmation.
What is the minimum deposit amount?
The minimum deposit is $10 USD equivalent in cryptocurrency. We recommend starting with at least $100 to manage risk effectively. Our Demo account requires no deposit at all and comes with $10,000 virtual funds.
How fast are withdrawals processed?
Withdrawal requests are processed within 24 hours on business days. Crypto withdrawals are typically sent within 2-4 hours once approved. There are no withdrawal fees charged by Velox — you only pay network fees.
What leverage is available?
We offer leverage up to 1:2000 on major Forex pairs, 1:500 on Gold, 1:200 on Indices, 1:100 on Stocks, and 1:50 on Cryptocurrencies. Higher leverage increases both potential profit and potential loss. Always use proper risk management.
Is KYC verification required?
KYC verification is required for withdrawals and for accounts with balances exceeding $500. You'll need to submit a government-issued ID (passport or national ID) and proof of address. Verification typically takes under 24 hours.
Which countries can use Velox?
Velox currently serves traders in Pakistan, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, Nepal, Bhutan, and the Maldives. We are expanding to additional regions. Clients from the United States, Canada, EU, Australia, Japan, and India cannot use the platform due to regulatory restrictions.
What trading instruments are available?
Velox offers 50+ instruments: 10+ Forex pairs, Gold and Silver, WTI and Brent Oil, Natural Gas, 20+ cryptocurrencies (BTC, ETH, SOL, BNB, XRP, ADA, and more), 9 global stock indices, and 20 US stocks including AAPL, TSLA, NVDA, MSFT, and META.
Does Velox charge any trading commissions?
Standard accounts have no commission — the cost is included in the spread. Raw Spread accounts charge $3.50 per lot per side in exchange for the tightest spreads available. There are no account maintenance fees, no inactivity fees, and no deposit fees.
